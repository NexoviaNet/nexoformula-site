Why most tools fall short

More summaries.
Not more understanding.

Most health tools reduce your experience to stats, tips, or generic advice. They can show you what happened, but not why things are shifting or what to change next.

What helps one person may do nothing for another. And what works for you can change over time.

The missing piece isn't more tracking. It's a way to learn what works for you.

A loop, not a score.
